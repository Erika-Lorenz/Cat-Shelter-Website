Adopt a Rescue Cat


If you have the home and the heart to make a furry little addition to your life, Cape-Atlantic C.A.T.S. will have the perfect match for you. Our facility cares for thousands of cats and kittens waiting to be your feline companion. Whether you prefer a kitten or two scurrying around the house or can provide a rural home for a neutered feral cat, we have every age, type and personality—all looking for their furever home.

The kittens and cats in our care come to us from overcrowded shelters and from our streets and campgrounds where they have been abandoned. They are examined by our veterinarian, de-wormed, FIV/FELV tested, receive age-appropriate vaccinations, and are spayed/neutered.
